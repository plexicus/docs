### Enable Two-Factor Authentication \{#settings_two-factor-authentication-1}

1. Navigate to [Authentication Settings](https://app.plexicus.ai/settings#authentication).
2. Click **Enable two-factor authentication**.
3. Follow the steps to set up two-factor authentication on your preferred authenticator app.
