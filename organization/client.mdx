## Configure Your Profile

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication

1. Navigate to [Authentication Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/settings#authentication).
2. Click **Enable two-factor authentication**.
3. Follow the steps to set up two-factor authentication on your preferred authenticator app.

### Change Your Password

1. Navigate to [Account Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/settings#account).
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter your new password and confirm it by entering it again.  
   **Password Requirements:**
   - Minimum of 8 characters.
   - At least one uppercase letter.
   - At least one special character.
   - At least one number.
4. Click the **Save User** button.

## Configure Your Organization

### Save Your Organization Name

1. Navigate to [Organization Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/organization).
2. Select the **Client** section.
3. Enter your organization's name.
4. Click the **Save Client** button.

### Change Your Plexicus Settings

#### Modify Your Schedule Scan

1. Navigate to [Organization Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/organization).
2. Select the **Covulor** section.
3. Choose your preferred schedule option:
   - None
   - Weekly
   - Monthly
4. Click the **Save** button.

#### Enable Advanced AI Analysis

1. Toggle the switch to enable **Advanced AI Analysis**.

#### Enable Prioritization Managed by Plexicus

1. Toggle the switch to enable **Prioritization Managed by Plexicus**.

### Add Members to Your Team

1. Navigate to [Team Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/organization).
2. Select the **Team** section.
3. Enter the new member's email in the input field.
4. Select their role:
   - **Developer**
   - **Cyberoper**
5. Click the **Create** button.

### Set Up Payment Methods

1. Navigate to [Billing Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/organization).
2. Select the **Billing** section.
3. Enter the following details:
   - Credit card information.
   - Address.
   - Legal name.
4. Click the **Save Billing** button.

### Make Changes to Your Azure Credentials

1. Navigate to [Azure OpenAI Settings](https://covulor.dev.plexicus.com/organization).
2. Select the **Azure OpenAI** section.
3. Enter the following details:
   - API Key.
   - API Version (optional).
   - Deployment Name.
   - Base URL.
